Physical Therapy program

"The physical therapy technician program helps students learn more in depth about the rehabilitation field. While the focus is on physical therapy, we also talk about occupational therapy, athletic training and other jobs in the medical field. A physical therapy technician helps assist a physical therapist in administering care to patients. That care is typically given via therapeutic exercise. A technician's role is to ensure that the exercise is completed properly with the correct modifications or progressions. Being a physical therapy technician is a very rewarding job assisting patients in tasks that they once could not do."

- Brittney Blady
